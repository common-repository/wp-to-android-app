Want to have a native android app for your site? here is the free and simplest solution for you. 

WP to Android is simply turn your website using <a href="http://appbuilder.kites.dev">Kites.Dev App builder</a> Service. 

== Description ==
 
Native mobile app for android platform, create a beautiful mobile app for your wordpress blog in minutes, no programming knowledge required.

= Features =
1. Control App from WordPress Admin Panel
1. Fully native performance 
1. Completely free to use.
1. Infinite Scroll
1. Customize App Colors as per your blog or company website.
1. Your own company Logo

 
== Installation ==
 
1. Upload the plugin to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to "Build Android App" Menu. 
1. Fill the form with your info. Click on the Build Button.
1. It may take some time to complete the build.
 
== Frequently Asked Questions ==
 
= Is it free forever? =
 
Yes! When you build the app you can use it forever. 

= Is it using any third party service? =
 
Yes! We use Spotlighht App builder Server to build your app. but when app build is the done, we don't have any access over app. 

= Do you collect any data? =
 
We collect your site url and your email address to our server. site url is used to build the app and your email is use to send you a update or email when your app build is done.

 
= Do i have full control? =
 
Yes! Your app is directly connected with your site. we don't have any access to your app after you build.
